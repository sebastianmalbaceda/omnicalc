# OmniCalc SaaS Design Plan ## 1. Landing Page (Web) - **Purpose:** Marketing and conversion. - **Key Sections:** Hero (CTA: "Use for Free"), Feature Cards (Sync, History, Precision), and a simple "Trusted by" social proof section. ## 2. Authentication (Login/Register) - **Purpose:** User onboarding. - **Design:** Centered card layout with OAuth (Google/GitHub) and email options. Focus on a "frictionless" feel. ## 3. Main Calculator (The Core App) - **Purpose:** Primary utility. - **Design:** Responsive layout. Desktop shows the 'Cloud Tape' (history) on the side. Pro users see the scientific panel integrated. - **Style:** Large, clear display with distinct colors for operators and numbers. ## 4. Pricing / Plans - **Purpose:** Monetization. - **Design:** Comparative table/cards for Free vs. Pro. Clear "Upgrade" triggers. ## 5. Settings & Profile - **Purpose:** Personalization and Account Management. - **Sections:** Profile details, Subscription status (Free/Pro badge), Theme switcher, and Haptic feedback toggle. ## 6. Help / Support - **Purpose:** Retention. - **Design:** FAQ section and a simple contact form.
